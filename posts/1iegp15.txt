A year ago I adopted an old cat named Mighty.  He was absolutely terrified of everything and everyone, had a kidney infection, a respiratory infection, an ear infection, moved with old aching bones, and didn't know how to meow. (He'd likely grown up feral in his early life and so never learned to do human audible meows). He wanted tummy rubs within an hour of us meeting though. It took almost 45 minutes of just sitting near him, but once he was comfy he wanted those belly rubs.

Now he wakes me up every morning with loud plaintive meows* for food and pets, gets zoomies on the regular, won't leave even strangers alone, naps on my desk with me, and has so much energy after I finally cleared his ear infection to properly.

*I started used little whining kitten meows to get him to come over and he eventually figured out he could do the same in return.

He's actually fiesty and nibbly sometimes now, and will want play for almost an hour before getting tired sometimes. Mighty is doing so well. <3
