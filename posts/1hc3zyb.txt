For example, we recently spent a whole week together without being intimate. When I tried initiating something, it didn’t work out. I’ve also noticed that he’s not affectionate in other ways either — no spontaneous “I love yous,” no compliments like “You’re beautiful,” no random kisses. I brought this up to him, crying, and told him how I feel like he’s emotionally distant, but he says there’s no problem. He insists that it’s just because his work has been more intense lately.
